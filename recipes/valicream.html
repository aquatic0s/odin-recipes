Vanilla Ice Cream

The classic vanilla flavored ice cream that everybody loves.

Ingredients:

  • 1 litre milk
  • 1/2 tablespoon corn flour
  • 1 cup fresh cream
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla essence

Steps:

  1. Boil the milk and keep stirring
  2. Add sugar to the blend
  3. Add fresh cream and vanilla essence
  4. Freeze and serve your hommade vanilla ice cream
  5. Tip: If you want to make it creamier, add whipped fresh cream
